100 Points of Interests Map

This map is produced from a dataset containing roughly 400,000 unique global points of interest. The map marks the first 100 places of interest from a kaggle points of interest dataset. The map allows users to explore points of interest.

The map has two modes: Map and Satellite (see Google Maps Platform documentation for more descrption). Clicking on the marker icon at each location will display the name of the point of interest, and it will also give some general contextual information about the location. Scroll out to see more global locations. Scroll to see more local locations. Placing the person icon on the map will allow users to explore street views of the location. Enjoy exploring these awesome points of interest!
